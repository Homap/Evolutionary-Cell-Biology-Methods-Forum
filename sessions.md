# Sessions

Each session follows one chapter of Michael Lynch’s *Evolutionary Cell Biology*.

For each chapter, we connect the book topic to two methodological themes:

1. a cell or molecular biology method
2. an evolutionary or comparative method

Each session page includes:

- the book chapter
- the main biological question
- a cell or molecular biology method theme
- an evolutionary or comparative method theme
- optional papers or resources
- discussion questions
- notes from the meeting

Specific papers and resources will be added closer to each session.
