# How to Join

Everyone in the department and beyond is welcome to join the Evolutionary Cell Biology Methods Forum.

You do not need to attend every session. Participants can join individual meetings based on interest.

## Format

Each session focuses on one chapter of Michael Lynch’s *Evolutionary Cell Biology* and two methodological themes:

1. a cell or molecular biology method
2. an evolutionary or comparative method

## Preparation

Before each session, participants are encouraged to read:

- the selected book chapter
- the short session description
- any optional papers or resources listed on the session page
