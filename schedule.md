# Schedule

The schedule will be updated throughout the reading group. Participants are welcome to join individual sessions.

Each session follows one chapter of Michael Lynch’s *Evolutionary Cell Biology*. For each chapter, we pair the book topic with two methodological themes:

1. a cell or molecular biology method
2. an evolutionary or comparative method

The challenge is to create a link between the two in each discussion session. The methodological topics suggested below are tentative and we can decide on specific topics together in each session.
