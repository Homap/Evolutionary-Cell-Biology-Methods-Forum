# Chapter 1 — Evolutionary Cell Biology

## Book chapter

Michael Lynch, *Evolutionary Cell Biology*, Chapter 1.

## Main question

What does it mean to study cell biology from an evolutionary perspective?

## Cell or molecular biology method theme

Quantitative cell phenotyping and image-based measurement.

## Evolutionary or comparative method theme

Comparative/evolutionary framing of cell biological traits.

## Possible discussion questions

1. What kinds of cell biological traits can be compared across species?
2. What is the difference between a mechanistic explanation and an evolutionary explanation?
3. How can quantitative imaging help turn cell biological observations into evolutionary traits?
4. What would an “evolutionary cell biology” research program look like in practice?
